We would expect that a large number of people are tweeting infrequently, and a small handful are tweeting magnitudes more.

Plots

Retweets contain a significant portion of the tweets made. To see how these trends change over time, the data set is split into quarters of the total time frame. The first plot contains retweets in the counts and the second does not. Also, due to the size of the data, the plots are of the log of the counts.
